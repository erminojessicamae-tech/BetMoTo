HOW TO PLAY

1. Pick a Category: Choose whether the final number will be PRIME (2, 3, 5...) or COMPOSITE (4, 6, 8...).

2. Set Amount: Enter your tokens manually or use the quick bet buttons.

3. Predict: Hit "CONFIRM BET" to lock in your choice and start the shuffle.

4. Win or Lose: If the randomly selected number matches your chosen category, you win up to 3x your stake!

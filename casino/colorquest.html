HOW TO PLAY

1. Pick Your Colors: Select one or more colors from the betting board.

2. Set Amount: Enter your bet amount per color manually or use the quick buttons.

3. Roll: Hit the "ROLL COLORS" button to drop the 3 dice into the tray.

4. Win or Lose: You win 3x your bet amount for every die that lands on your chosen color(s)!

HOW TO PLAY

1. Place a Bet: Enter your tokens manually or use the quick bet buttons.

2. Deal Cards: Hit "DEAL CARDS" to receive your starting hand.

3. Hit or Stand: Try to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over (Busting). Click HIT to draw another card, or STAND to hold your total.

4. Win or Lose: Beat the dealer's score to win! A winning hand grants you 3x your stake.

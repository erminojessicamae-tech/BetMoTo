HOW TO PLAY

1. Pick a Bet: Choose BIG (Sum 8-12), SMALL (Sum 2-6), or DOUBLE (Matching dice like 4 & 4).

2. Set Amount: Enter your tokens manually or use the quick bet buttons.

3. Roll: Hit "ROLL DICE" to shake the dice and reveal the sum.

4. Win or Lose: Win 2x your stake for Big/Small, or a massive 5x for guessing a Double!

(Note: A sum of 7 is a House win).
